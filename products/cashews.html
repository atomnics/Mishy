The Origin Story

The Mtwara region in southeastern Tanzania is Africa's premier cashew-producing territory. The sandy coastal soils, reliable rainfall patterns, and warm tropical climate create ideal conditions for cashew trees to thrive, producing nuts with exceptional kernel quality and recovery rates.

Our processing partners operate state-of-the-art facilities that employ thousands of skilled workers, combining traditional hand-processing techniques with modern quality control systems to deliver consistently premium kernels.

Quality Grades

W180 Premium W240 Standard W320 Popular Splits & Pieces

All grades undergo rigorous sorting and quality control. W180 represents the largest, most premium whole kernels, while W240 and W320 offer excellent value for snacking and food manufacturing. Processed kernels are available roasted, salted, or plain.
