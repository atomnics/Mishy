The Origin Story

Grown in the fertile volcanic soils surrounding Mount Meru, Arusha's coffee has earned international acclaim for its distinctive flavor profile. Our partner cooperatives in the Arusha region follow traditional shade-growing practices, cultivating coffee beneath native forest canopy that preserves biodiversity while producing exceptional beans.

The unique microclimate of the northern highlands—cool nights, warm days, and reliable rainfall—creates ideal conditions for slow cherry maturation, concentrating the sugars and complex acids that define premium specialty coffee.

Flavor Profile

Bright Citrus Dark Chocolate Blackcurrant Floral Notes Wine-like Acidity

Arusha coffees are prized for their complex acidity and clean, bright cup character. Expect notes of bergamot and citrus on the front palate, transitioning to rich chocolate and berry undertones with a lingering, wine-like finish.
